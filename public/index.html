About

A practitioner at the intersection

Memento Consultancy is the independent practice of Søren Poulsen, a computer scientist and educator based in Aarhus, Denmark, with a long-standing connection to Aarhus University.

My work sits at the intersection of research, education and technology — a space where curiosity, rigour and real-world pragmatism all need to coexist. I work with companies seeking academic partnerships, with institutions building better bridges to their future students, and with small ventures figuring out what AI can actually do for them.

Engagements are kept deliberately small and personal. You work directly with me — not a team of junior consultants — which means focused attention, honest advice, and no unnecessary overhead.
